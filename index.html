Matte Black & Unlacquered Brass

Brushed nickel and chrome are out of Texas luxury homes. Matte black faucets, cabinet pulls, light fixtures, and door hardware are the 2026 standard — often paired with unlacquered brass on islands for contrast. We source from Kohler, Brizo, and Watermark for finishes that hold up in Texas humidity.

Arched Doorways & Alcoves

The curved arch is the single most requested architectural detail in 2026 Texas remodeling — replacing square pass-throughs, framing window seats, and defining open-plan zones without walls. We frame and plaster arched openings as part of kitchen expansions and whole-home remodels. Pairs perfectly with shiplap and board-and-batten.

Fluted Wood & Reeded Glass Panels

Vertical reeding — on cabinet fronts, kitchen islands, bath vanities, and room dividers — is replacing flat surfaces with tactile texture. Fluted white oak, walnut, or painted MDF adds dimension without bulk. Reeded glass inserts in upper cabinet doors create warmth and visual interest while keeping contents softly visible.
