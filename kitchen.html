Scope of Work

Every kitchen scenario we handle.

01 — Renovation

Full Kitchen Renovation

Complete gut-and-rebuild. New layout if you want it, custom cabinetry, countertops, backsplash, lighting, plumbing, and appliance connections. We manage every trade in-house — you deal with one crew, one timeline, one point of contact from demo to final walkthrough.

02 — Island

Custom Island Build

An island transforms how a kitchen functions — additional prep space, seating, storage, and a visual anchor for the room. We design and build custom islands with waterfall countertops, integrated appliances, pendant lighting rough-in, and seating overhangs sized to your specific dimensions.

03 — Cabinetry

Cabinet Refacing & Replacement

If your cabinet boxes are solid but the doors and finish are dated, refacing can give you a completely new look at significantly lower cost than full replacement. When boxes need to go, we build and install custom cabinetry to exact dimensions — no stock sizing compromises.

04 — Countertops

Countertop Replacement

Quartz, quartzite, marble, granite, concrete, butcher block — we template, fabricate, and install. Countertop replacement paired with new hardware and a backsplash can refresh a kitchen without the cost of a full remodel. We coordinate the plumbing disconnects and reconnects in-house.

05 — Tile

Backsplash & Tile Work

From simple subway tile to full-height slab backsplashes, hand-painted zellige, herringbone patterns, or dimensional stone — we install it all. Our tile crews work with the same precision whether the job is a 15-square-foot backsplash or a floor-to-ceiling statement wall.

06 — Expansion

Kitchen Expansion

When the kitchen is just too small to work with, we evaluate structural options for opening it up — removing walls (load-bearing or not), borrowing space from adjacent rooms, or adding square footage through a bump-out. We handle permits, structural engineering referrals, and full build.
