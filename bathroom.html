Our Scope of Work

Every bathroom scenario, covered.

01

Tub-to-Shower Conversions

Remove that builder-grade fiberglass tub and replace it with a curbless walk-in shower — large-format tile, frameless glass enclosure, linear drain, and rain head. This is our most-requested upgrade and one of the best investments you can make in a Texas home.

02

Full Master Suite Remodel

Complete gut-and-rebuild of your master bathroom. New layout when needed, custom tile work, soaking tub, steam shower, heated floors, dual vanity with custom cabinetry, and full lighting design. We manage every trade from demo to final walkthrough — one crew, one point of contact.

03

Shower Modernization

Tile surrounds cracking, grout discoloring, glass frames corroding — these aren't cosmetic problems, they're water intrusion waiting to happen. We remove the old surround, apply Schluter waterproofing, and install a tile system built to last 30 years. Walk-in niches, bench seating, and multi-head setups available.

04

Vanity & Fixture Upgrades

A floating vanity, backlit mirror, quartz countertop, and updated plumbing fixtures can transform a bathroom in a week without a full renovation. We handle demo, tile patching, plumbing, and electrical as one crew — no subcontractor coordination on your part.

05

Powder Room Transformation

The room every guest sees. Statement wallcovering, vessel sink, dramatic floor tile, distinctive lighting — a powder room is where you take design risks you might not take elsewhere. Small footprint, outsized impression. We make them the most memorable room in the house.

06

Secondary & Jack-and-Jill Baths

Secondary bathrooms see constant traffic and rarely get attention. We renovate them to match the quality of your primary spaces — durable large-format tile, quality fixtures, properly waterproofed showers, and smart storage. Done right, they're maintenance-free for two decades.
