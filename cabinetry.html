What we do

Every inch
accounted for.

Stock cabinets fill space. Custom cabinets use it. We build to the exact dimensions of your room — capturing every inch of a 9-foot ceiling, filling that odd-angle corner, and making the refrigerator panel look like it was always there.

All cabinet boxes are built in our shop from 3/4" plywood — not particleboard. Doors are solid wood or MDF with closed-cell construction. Hardware is Blum or Grass, full stop.

How it works

Our process is designed to eliminate surprises. You know what's happening, when, and why — before we ever swing a hammer.

01

Field Measure

We measure the space to 1/16" accuracy, noting every out-of-square wall, soffit, and ceiling variation.

02

3D Design Review

You review a photo-realistic 3D rendering of the cabinetry before a single board is cut.

03

Shop Fabrication

Boxes, doors, and drawers built in our shop over 3–4 weeks, with a quality check before delivery.

04

Install & Adjust

Cabinets installed plumb and level, doors aligned, hardware set, and panels scribed to walls.
