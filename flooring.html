Flooring Types

Luxury Vinyl Plank

The most popular flooring choice in Houston homes today — and for good reason. LVP gives you the look of real wood with 100% waterproof performance, making it ideal for kitchens, bathrooms, and open-plan living areas. Modern LVP is virtually indistinguishable from hardwood at a fraction of the cost.

  • 100% waterproof — safe for bathrooms and kitchens
  • 6–8mm thick with attached underlayment for comfort and sound dampening
  • Click-lock floating installation over most existing substrates
  • Dent and scratch resistant — built for family life
  • Available in 5"–9" plank widths and 50+ colorways
  • Typical project: 2–3 days for a full home

Why It Matters

The floor you see is only half the story.

Most flooring failures trace back to substrate problems — moisture, deflection, unlevel concrete, or inadequate adhesive. We start every flooring project with a proper site assessment: moisture readings, flatness measurements, and subfloor inspection. The installation you see reflects the prep work you don't.

  • Moisture barrier installation standard on all slab-on-grade projects
  • Self-leveling compound used where flatness exceeds 3/16" in 10 feet
  • Subfloor squeaks addressed before any finish material goes down
  • Transition strips and thresholds at every doorway, no raw edges
  • Furniture removal and reinstallation available on request

Our Scope

Every flooring scenario we handle.

01

Whole-Home Flooring

Coordinate your flooring across every room for a unified look. We plan transitions between zones — wood to tile, tile to carpet, level changes — so the flow feels intentional, not piecemeal. One crew, one schedule, one point of contact.

02

Subfloor Repair & Prep

Squeaky subfloors, soft spots, moisture damage, uneven concrete — we fix the foundation before any finish material touches it. Proper prep is the difference between a floor that looks great for 20 years and one that starts failing in 3.

03

Bathroom & Wet Area Tile

Floor tile in wet areas requires specific substrates, waterproofing membranes, and proper slope to drain. We install shower floors with precise pitch, bathroom tile with anti-slip finishes, and coordinate wall and floor tile to create a cohesive design.

04

Hardwood Refinishing

Existing hardwood that's dull, scratched, or stained can often be brought back to like-new condition through sanding, staining, and refinishing. We assess whether your existing floors can be saved — which can be significantly more cost-effective than replacement.

05

Stair Flooring

Stairs are one of the most technically demanding flooring installs. We cover treads and risers with hardwood, LVP, or carpet — with properly nosed edges, safe traction, and clean returns. Matching your stair material to your adjoining rooms is something we plan from the start.

06

Outdoor & Patio Tile

Houston's climate demands specific materials for outdoor flooring — porcelain rated for freeze-thaw, slip-resistant finishes, and proper drainage slope. We install covered patio tile, pool surrounds, and front entry tile that holds up to Texas heat, humidity, and rain.

Our Process

From assessment to final trim.

Flooring is more than dropping planks. Here's how we approach every project to ensure long-term performance.

01

Site Assessment

Moisture readings, flatness checks, subfloor inspection. We identify issues before they become problems and give you a complete picture of what the project actually involves.

02

Material Selection

We connect you with our supplier network at trade pricing. You see samples in your actual lighting before anything is ordered. Pattern and layout are finalized before the first board goes down.

03

Prep & Installation

Existing flooring removed, subfloor addressed, material acclimated to your home's humidity. Installation proceeds by zone with daily progress updates and protection of completed areas.

04

Trim & Cleanup

Transitions, thresholds, base molding reinstalled or replaced. Full cleanup including dust from any sanding. Final walkthrough before we consider the job done.
